Smoothie

Description

Combine fresh or frozen fruits (e.g., berries, banana, mango) with yogurt or milk (dairy or plant-based) in a blender. Add a sweetener like honey or maple syrup if desired. Blend until smooth and creamy. Pour into a glass, and enjoy a refreshing and nutritious smoothie.

Ingredients

  • Fresh or frozen fruits (e.g., strawberries, blueberries, raspberries, banana, mango, etc.)
  • Yogurt (plain or flavored) or Milk (dairy or plant-based)
  • Sweetener (e.g., honey, maple syrup) (optional)

Steps

  1. Choose your favorite combination of fresh or frozen fruits. For example, you can use a mix of berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries) or tropical fruits like banana and mango.
  2. In a blender, add the fruits and a dollop of yogurt or pour in your choice of milk (dairy or plant-based) to create the base of the smoothie.
  3. If you prefer a sweeter taste, add a drizzle of honey or maple syrup as per your liking
  4. Close the blender securely and blend all the ingredients until the mixture turns smooth and creamy.
  5. If you find the smoothie too thick, you can add more liquid (yogurt or milk) to achieve your desired consistency.
  6. Once the smoothie is well-blended, pour it into a glass and enjoy the refreshing and nutritious treat.
