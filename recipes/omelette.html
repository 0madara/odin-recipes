Omelette

Description

Whisk eggs with a splash of milk, salt, and pepper. Heat butter in a non-stick pan. Pour in the egg mixture. Add desired fillings like cheese, veggies, or ham. Cook until set, then fold in half. Serve hot with a sprinkle of fresh herbs.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter
  • Cheese (optional)
  • Veggies (e.g., onions, bell peppers, tomatoes) (optional)
  • Ham (optional)
  • Fresh herbs (e.g., parsley, chives) for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Crack eggs into a bowl and whisk with a splash of milk, a pinch of salt, and a dash of pepper.
  2. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and add a pat of butter to melt.
  3. Pour the egg mixture into the pan, spreading it evenly.
  4. Add desired fillings like cheese, diced veggies, or chopped ham on one side of the omelette.
  5. Once the eggs are mostly set, carefully fold the omelette in half using a spatula.
  6. Let it cook for a minute or so until the fillings are warmed through, and the omelette is fully set
  7. Slide the omelette onto a plate and garnish with fresh herbs if desired.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious homemade omelette!
